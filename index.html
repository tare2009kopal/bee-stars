About the Program

Taught by someone who's stood at the mic.

Bee Stars was founded by Kopal Tare, a high school junior at Dublin High School who has competed — and placed — at the school, district, county, and state levels of spelling bee competition.

Having navigated the nerves, the study routines, and the strategies that actually work, Kopal built Bee Stars to share everything she's learned — completely free — with the next generation of spellers.
