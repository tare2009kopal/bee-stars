About Bee Stars

Founded on passion.
Built for young learners.

Bee Stars is a community-driven spelling tutoring program founded on a deep passion for language and a commitment to student success.

My name is Kopal Tare — a junior at Dublin High School and a competitive speller with placements at the school, district, county, and state levels. Through Bee Stars, I bring firsthand experience directly to students, offering structured instruction in the same skills that shaped my own journey.

My goal is simple: give every young learner the skills, confidence, and preparation they need to thrive in spelling bees and beyond — one word at a time.

FAQ

Frequently asked
questions.

What happens during a weekly session?

Each session opens with an in-depth lesson on a core topic — such as diacritics, etymology, or phonetics — followed by interactive practice activities including spelling rounds, word games, and exercises. Sessions are structured to be both rigorous and engaging.

Where are sessions held?

All sessions are conducted online via Zoom. Login details are sent out after you register — no setup required on your end.

How long is the program?

The program runs for 10 weeks, with one 30-minute session per week on Sundays from 5:30–6:00 PM PST. It takes place annually from November through January.

Is there a cost to participate?

Bee Stars is entirely free of charge — always. No hidden fees, no materials cost, no subscription.

Who can join?

The program is designed for students in Grades K–7, though all motivated learners are welcome regardless of grade level or prior spelling bee experience.
