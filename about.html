Founder & Instructor

Kopal Tare

Dublin High School · State-Level Speller · Community Educator

I started Bee Stars because I remember exactly how it felt to stand at a microphone in front of judges, with a word I'd never heard before. I remember the way etymology clicked — the moment I realized that if I knew the Latin root, I could spell the word without ever having seen it.

I want every student who walks into a spelling bee to feel that same confidence. That's why I built this program, and why I teach it completely free.

The Mission

Why free? Why now?

Access to quality spelling bee coaching has traditionally required expensive tutors or specialized programs. Bee Stars was built to change that — every student, regardless of background, deserves the same preparation.

Sessions cover not just word lists, but the underlying systems: etymology, phonetics, diacritics, and linguistics. These tools don't just help you spell — they help you understand language.

Over two years and two cohorts, Bee Stars has helped students win their first spelling bees, advance to higher rounds, and — most importantly — develop a genuine love for words.
